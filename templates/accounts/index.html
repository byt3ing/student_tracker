Welcome to Debadhwani Kolkata

Celebrating the art of Dance & Drawing – nurturing creativity, discipline, and culture for all age groups.

About Us

Debadhwani Kolkata is a cultural academy dedicated to teaching classical dance and fine arts. We believe in preserving tradition while encouraging modern creativity in our students.

Our Classes

Dance: Kathak, Creative & Folk Dance (Mon/Wed/Fri, 5–7 PM).

Drawing: Beginner to Advanced Sketching & Painting (Sat/Sun, 11 AM–1 PM).

Our Guru

Led by Sangita Ghosh, a passionate artist with 15+ years of experience in classical dance, guiding students with love and discipline.

Exams & Certification

Students can appear for nationally recognized exams in dance and drawing. Successful candidates are awarded certificates from Sarvabharatiya.
